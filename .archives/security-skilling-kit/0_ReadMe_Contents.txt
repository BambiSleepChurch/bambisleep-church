Microsoft Security Skilling Kit – What’s inside

This kit brings together key resources to help you launch and guide security training in your organization. Files are grouped by steps referenced in the Get Started guide.

1 – Awareness: Resources to help you explain why security skilling matters and share with leaders or employees.

- Get Started Guide (1-GetStartedGuide_ReadMeFirst.pdf)
- Whitepaper: Stay Ahead of Cyberthreats
- Whitepaper: Skilling for secure AI
- One-Pager: Why Security Skilling

2 – Training: Resources to help you guide employees to the right training options.

- Microsoft Security Technical Training Poster
- Learning Paths: Cloud, AI, and App Security; Data Security; Modern SecOps

3 – Ongoing Learning: Sustain progress with planning, reporting, and additional resources.

- Organization Skilling Playbook: A step-by-step guide for training managers and group leads. Download at aka.ms/LearnForOrgs
- Microsoft Learn Security Hub: learn.microsoft.com/security
