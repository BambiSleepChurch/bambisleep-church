2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and authentication credentials when connecting to third-party services (GitHub, Stripe, Patreon, HuggingFace).
  • Configuration Data: MCP server configurations, API keys, and environment variables you configure.
  • Knowledge Graph Data: Entities, relations, and observations you create in the memory system.
  • Chat Data: Messages and conversations with the AI agent system.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: We use Microsoft Clarity to collect anonymized usage data including page views, clicks, scroll behavior, and session recordings.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device identifiers.
  • Log Data: Server logs including IP addresses, access times, and API request metadata.
  • WebSocket Data: Real-time connection status and event data for dashboard updates.

2.3 Third-Party Service Data

When you connect third-party services, we may receive:

  • GitHub: Repository information, issue data, commit history, and user profile data.
  • Stripe: Customer IDs, subscription status, and payment metadata (not payment card details).
  • Patreon: Campaign information, member data, tier details, and webhook events.
  • HuggingFace: Model usage data and inference results.
  • MongoDB: Database connection metadata and query logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Operation: To provide, maintain, and improve the MCP Control Tower functionality.
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and manage access to connected services.
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve user experience.
  • Communication: To send service-related notifications and respond to inquiries.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most data processed by the MCP Control Tower is stored locally on your infrastructure. This includes:

  • Knowledge graph data stored in local JSON files
  • SQLite database files
  • Uploaded files in the storage system
  • Configuration files and environment variables

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • CORS restrictions for API access
  • Secure WebSocket connections
  • Environment variable encryption for sensitive credentials
  • Regular security updates and patches

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity for analytics. Clarity may collect data including mouse movements, clicks, and scroll behavior. For more information, see Microsoft Clarity Terms.

5.2 Connected APIs

When you connect external services, your data is also subject to their privacy policies:

  • GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Patreon Privacy Policy
  • HuggingFace Privacy Policy

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing.
  • Service Providers: With third-party services you choose to connect.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, legal process, or government request.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Portability: Export your knowledge graph and configuration data.
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics tracking by blocking Microsoft Clarity in your browser.
  • Disconnect: Revoke access to connected third-party services at any time.

8. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Locally stored data persists until you delete it. Analytics data is retained according to Microsoft Clarity's retention policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

If you access our services from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our servers or third-party services are located.
